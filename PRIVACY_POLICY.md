# Privacy Policy for ScannrExpense

Last updated: 2026-06-14

ScannrExpense helps you scan, organize, and export receipt and expense information on your iPhone and through Apple's iCloud services.

Contact: support@scannrfast.se

## Data the app handles

ScannrExpense may process receipt photos, PDFs, scanned text, expense notes, amounts, dates, vendor names, export packages, subscription purchase state, and app settings that you enter or import into the app.

## Storage and sync

Your receipt and expense data is stored on your device and may sync through your personal iCloud account when iCloud features are enabled. iCloud storage and sync are provided by Apple and are governed by Apple's [terms](https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/icloud/) and [privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We do not operate any server that receives or stores a copy of your receipt or expense data.

## Purchases

Subscription purchases are handled by Apple through StoreKit. ScannrExpense can read purchase status needed to unlock subscription features, but payment processing is handled by Apple.

## What we collect

ScannrExpense does not collect personal data on our own servers. All content you create stays on your device and, optionally, in your personal iCloud account. This maps to the App Store privacy label category "Data Not Collected" with respect to data held by us.

## Legal basis for processing

To the extent any processing involves us as a controller, we rely on the performance of our agreement with you to provide the app's features (GDPR Article 6(1)(b)) and, where applicable, our legitimate interest in operating and improving the app (Article 6(1)(f)).

## Data retention

Because your data is stored on your device and in your own iCloud account, it is retained until you delete it. We do not keep copies and therefore have no separate retention period for your receipt or expense data.

## Account and data deletion

ScannrExpense does not create or maintain any developer-side user account. All receipt and expense data is stored locally on your device and, if you enable iCloud, synced privately through your own Apple iCloud account — we never receive or store a copy on our servers. You can delete all of your data at any time by removing the items within the app, deleting the app from your device, and removing the app's data from your iCloud account (Settings > [your name] > iCloud > Manage Account Storage). Because we hold no personal data about you, there is no developer account to delete; subscription billing is handled entirely by Apple.

## Your rights

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have rights under the GDPR, including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data, and the right to data portability. As described above, your content is held by you on your device and in your iCloud account, so you can exercise most of these rights directly. For any request concerning data we control, contact us at support@scannrfast.se.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority. In Sweden this is the Swedish Authority for Privacy Protection (Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten, IMY), https://www.imy.se.

## Children

ScannrExpense is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 16.

## Tracking and advertising

ScannrExpense does not use advertising tracking and does not sell your personal data.

## Support contact

For privacy or support questions, contact: support@scannrfast.se
